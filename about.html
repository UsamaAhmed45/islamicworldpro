About

Built to be one quiet app, not a dozen loud ones.

Islamic World Pro started from a simple frustration: daily worship shouldn't require switching between a Qur'an app, a prayer times app, a Qibla app, and a Tasbih app — each with its own ads, its own account, its own interruptions.

The idea

Everything you actually use, nothing you don't

Every tool inside Islamic World Pro maps to something people already do — read Qur'an, track five prayers, find the Qibla, recite Azkar, count dhikr, check the Hijri date, calculate Zakat, prepare for Hajj or Umrah, teach children the basics, or find the Janaza prayer when it's needed most. Nothing here is decoration; each feature earns its place because it's part of that daily or seasonal rhythm.

The app is also built to work offline once loaded, because a moment of prayer or reading shouldn't depend on signal strength.

What guides the design

  • No clutter, no distractions — just the essentials
  • Reliable notifications, even in the background
  • Works offline once loaded
  • A calm, dark, distraction-free interface
In short

One app. Thirteen tools. Five daily prayers to build around.

Qur'an, Prayer Times, Qibla, Azkar, Dua, Tasbih, Hijri Calendar, Hadith, Zakat, Hajj & Umrah, Kids Corner, Janaza, and daily reminders — designed to work together, not compete for space on your home screen.
