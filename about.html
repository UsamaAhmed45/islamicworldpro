Our Story

About Islamic World Pro

A small team building a calm, reliable Islamic companion — for the five prayers, the Qur'an, and everything in between.

Our mission

Make daily practice easier, not busier

Islamic World Pro was built by Aurevia Solution with one goal: bring the Qur'an, Azkar, Hadith, prayer times and Hajj & Umrah guidance into a single, uncluttered place — so opening the app feels like a moment of calm, not another notification to manage.

We keep the design quiet, the Arabic text legible, and the content sourced carefully, so families, students and anyone building a daily habit of worship can rely on it.

What guides us

Our principles

Accuracy first

Qur'an text, translations, tafseer and Hadith are drawn from established, widely trusted sources.

Calm by design

No clutter, no aggressive notifications — just what you came to read.

Built for everyone

From a first-time reader of the Qur'an to someone preparing for Hajj, the app meets you where you are.
