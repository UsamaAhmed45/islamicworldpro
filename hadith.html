Well-known narrations

Sahih al-Bukhari & Muslim

Every deed is weighed by the intention behind it, and each person is rewarded according to what they intended — so a person whose migration was for Allah and His Messenger, that is what their migration counts for; and one who migrated for worldly gain or marriage, their migration counts for only that.

Narrated by 'Umar ibn al-Khattab (RA)

Sahih Muslim

None of you truly believes until he wants for his brother what he wants for himself.

Narrated by Anas ibn Malik (RA)

Sahih al-Bukhari

Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day should speak good or remain silent, should honour their neighbour, and should honour their guest.

Narrated by Abu Hurairah (RA)

Jami at-Tirmidhi

The most complete believers in faith are those with the best character, and the best among you are those who are best to their wives.

Narrated by Abu Hurairah (RA)
