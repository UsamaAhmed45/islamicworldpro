Everything inside the app

Thirteen tools built around one daily rhythm

Every feature in Islamic World Pro exists to support the same five moments a day — not to compete for your attention.

01 · Reading

Qur'an, with Tajweed built in

A clean Mushaf-style layout across all 30 Juz, with Tajweed rules colour-coded directly into the script — so correct recitation becomes visible, not just something you're told to remember.

02 · Prayer & Direction

Prayer times and Qibla, calculated for exactly where you are

Fajr through Isha, recalculated for your precise location, with reliable Adhan notifications — including the background alarm fixes needed to keep them firing on modern Android. The Qibla compass uses your device's sensors and GPS, and still works with a weak signal.

See Prayer & Qibla in detail
03 · Remembrance

Azkar — morning, evening & sleep

Structured daily remembrance sets for morning and evening, plus a dedicated Sleep Azkar list for the end of the day — each formatted for easy, unhurried reading.

04 · Supplication

Dua collection

A curated library of duas for everyday situations, organised so you can find the right one quickly, without scrolling through everything else.

05 · Dhikr

Tasbih counter

A built-in digital counter for dhikr, with haptic feedback and a simple reset — no need to keep a separate counting app installed.

06 · Calendar

Hijri calendar

Islamic dates and events sit alongside the Gregorian calendar, so upcoming occasions never catch you by surprise.

07 · Study

Hadith, close at hand

Browse authentic Hadith collections in the same place you read Qur'an and Azkar — so daily study doesn't mean jumping between four different apps.

"The best among you are those who learn the Qur'an and teach it."

Sahih al-Bukhari

Beyond the daily rhythm

The moments that don't come every day

Giving, pilgrimage, teaching your children, and moments of loss — each has its place in the app, not bolted on as an afterthought.

08 · Giving

Zakat Calculator

Enter your cash, gold, and savings and the calculator applies the Nisab threshold automatically, so you always know exactly what's due.

09 · Pilgrimage

Hajj & Umrah Guide

Every rite in order — Ihram to Tawaf al-Wida — with Arabic, transliteration, Urdu and English side by side.

10 · For the family

Kids Corner

Short surahs to learn, everyday dua cards, illustrated Prophet stories, and a quiz for children.

11 · When it's needed

Janaza Guide

The funeral prayer and surrounding rites, laid out clearly for a moment when clarity matters most.

12 · Staying consistent

Five reminders, spaced through your day

Fixed daily alarms keep you on track even if you never open the app that day — each one tied to a specific moment.

Morning

Morning Azkar

A gentle nudge to start the day with remembrance.

Midday

Ayah of the Day

One verse, delivered as a quiet pause in the middle of your day.

Afternoon

Hadith of the Day

A short, authentic Hadith worth carrying with you.

Evening

Qur'an Reading Reminder

A prompt to pick up where you left off.

Night

Evening & Sleep Azkar

Closing remembrance before rest.

13 · Offline, always

Everything above works without a connection

Load it once, and Islamic World Pro keeps working — on a flight, underground, or anywhere signal doesn't reach.
