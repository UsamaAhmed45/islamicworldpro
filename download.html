Getting started

Three steps once you have the app

Step 1

Allow location (optional)

This gives you accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for exactly where you are. If you skip it, the app still works — it simply defaults to Makkah until you enable it.

Step 2

Turn on notifications

Needed for Adhan alerts and your daily reminders. On Android 12 and newer, also approve the exact-alarm and battery permissions when prompted, so alerts keep firing in the background.

Step 3

Load it once, then go offline

Open the Qur'an, Azkar, Hadith, and other content sections once while connected — after that, everything is available without signal.

Requirements

Android 7.0 or newer

Islamic World Pro runs on Android 7.0 (API 24) and above, on phones, tablets, and Chrome OS devices.

Questions

Before you download

Is Islamic World Pro free?

Yes — it's free to download from Google Play.

Does it need internet to work?

Only the first time you open each section. After that, the Qur'an, Azkar, Dua, Hadith, Zakat, Hajj & Umrah, and Kids Corner content is available offline.

Which devices are supported?

Any Android phone, tablet, or Chrome OS device running Android 7.0 or newer.
